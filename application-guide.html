BYU-Pathway Worldwide Application Guide

1. To apply to BYU-Pathway Worldwide, you first need to create a path account. To do that, visit https://churchofjesuschrist.org and click sign in.

2. Click "Create a new Account" on the "Sign In page" and follow the instructions to create an account. Be sure to remember your username and password.

3. Visit https://byupathway.edu and click "Apply For Free".

3. Click "Apply Now".
